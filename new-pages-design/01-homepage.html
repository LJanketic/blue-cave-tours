Common questions

All FAQs →

What happens if the weather is bad?

We monitor conditions closely. If a tour is cancelled due to weather, you'll receive a full refund or the option to rebook at no extra cost. We'll notify you at least 4 hours before departure.

Do I need to know how to swim?

Not at all. Life jackets are provided on all tours. Swimming stops are optional — you're welcome to stay on the boat.

Can I bring young children?

Yes — children aged 3 and above are welcome on all group tours. Life jackets are available in children's sizes. Private charters have no minimum age.

How do I cancel or reschedule?

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before departure. Inside 48 hours, bookings are non-refundable. To reschedule, contact us and we'll do our best to find an alternative date.

Ready to get on the water?

Tours depart daily. Most sell out 3–5 days ahead in summer — book early.
