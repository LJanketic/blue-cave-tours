1. Bookings

A booking is confirmed once full payment (group tours) or the required deposit (private charters) has been received and a confirmation email has been sent. You must be 18 or older to make a booking.

Changes to guest numbers or departure date are subject to availability and must be requested at least 48 hours before departure.

2. Payment

Group tours are payable in full at the time of booking. Private charters require a non-refundable deposit of 30% at booking, with the balance due 7 days before departure.

If the balance for a private charter is not received 7 days before departure, Adriatic Sails reserves the right to cancel the booking and retain the deposit.

All payments are processed securely via Stripe. Prices are quoted in Euros and include all applicable taxes.

3. Guest conduct

All guests must follow the instructions of the captain and crew at all times. The captain has the right to refuse boarding or remove a guest if their behaviour poses a risk to the safety or enjoyment of others.

Guests removed for conduct reasons are not entitled to a refund, partial or otherwise.

4. Liability

Adriatic Sails holds full public liability insurance covering all passengers. We are not liable for loss or damage to personal belongings brought on board. Nothing in these terms limits liability for death or personal injury caused by negligence.

5. Safety

All vessels are inspected and certified in accordance with Croatian Maritime Authority requirements. The captain's decision regarding sailing conditions is final.

6. Governing law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Croatia. Disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Dubrovnik-Neretva County.

3. Weather cancellations

If Adriatic Sails cancels a tour due to unsafe conditions, you receive either a full refund (within 5–10 business days) or a free rebook to any available date within the same season — your choice.

We will notify you at least 4 hours before the scheduled departure in the event of a weather cancellation.

4. Refunds

Eligible refunds are returned to the original payment method within 5–10 business days. Refunds are issued in Euros; we are not liable for exchange rate fluctuations.

5. How to cancel

To cancel, contact us with your booking reference via email (hello@adriaticsails.com), WhatsApp (+385 91 234 5678), or phone (+385 20 123 456).

Keep a record of when you sent your cancellation request. We recommend using email so you have a timestamp in case of any dispute.
